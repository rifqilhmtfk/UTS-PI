About

Jamie Ittihadul Umami Mosque is a mosque that has a unique and impressive history

The Jamie Ittihadul Umami Mosque not only functions as a place of worship, but also as a center for education and social activities. This mosque often holds recitations and other charity activities. This mosque is also a gathering place for the people of Kp. Lulurung to establish friendship and strengthen the unity of the Muslim community. The Jamie Ittihadul Umami Mosque also has a unique history because before it became a mosque in the 1970s, the place was often used for children to recite the Koran. However, after the owner of the house died, the place was used as a waqf for the construction of a mosque, and to this day the mosque still stands
